Article One

From each as they will to each as they've given so all may eat save those refusing to contribute to their comfort and safety or whose actions destroy the comfort and safety of another.

Notes & Mnemonics
  • A cry sharpens vigilance. Fragility elicits innovation.
  • Watch for: paper tigers, malingerers, Greek gifts, thieves, narcissists, empty calories, liars.

Article Two

None shall be enslaved; do nothing against your will, but feed the hungry, heal the sick, teach the kids, clean the mess, learn the Logics, cope the traumas, practice your art.

Low-density version available in canon for accessibility.

Article Five — Adulthood & Interdependence

Adulthood is claimed by demonstrated competence. Independence = survival skill-set. Interdependence = political adulthood (consequences beyond self).

Milestone: firecraft, cooking, first aid, sanitation, communication, maintenance.
