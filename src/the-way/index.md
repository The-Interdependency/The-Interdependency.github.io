The Interdependent Way is a set of Articles, definitions, and addenda that specify duties, competencies, and the conditions for just interaction. Each page provides: a plain‑language lead, canonical claims, examples, misunderstandings, practice guidance, and sources.

Reading path
1. Articles I–III — duties and boundaries.
2. Articles IV–VI — knowledge, adulthood, and authority.
3. Articles VII–VIII — definition and law.
4. Etiquette of the Body Politic and Addenda.
