Preamble

Humanity faces challenges from incompetence and dishonesty in authority. The Interdependent Way proposes principles for survival and thriving through competence and duty. [Theory: Consciousness emerges from triadic systems, citing Hofstadter (1979).] [Citation: Locke, Tho Book of Government (1689).]

Core Principles

  • Article One: From each as they will, to each as they've given. [Citation: Natural law precedents. ]
  • Article Two: None shall be enslaved; do nothing against your will, yet feed the hungry, heal the sick. [Theory: Balance of natural systems.]
