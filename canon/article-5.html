Article Five

Claiming Adulthood Through Demonstrated Competence

Core Statement: Adulthood ceases to be defined by an arbitrary age and is instead claimed by intentional action of demonstrated competence in a field of study. Independence is the field describing the general skill-set to obtain relief from parental or guardian limitations. An interdependent adult owns the consequences of their existence beyond being held accountable for deliberate or negligent harm.

Tensor Interpretation: Activates the competence spiral in Mind(Body)Soul and I(You)We. Moves individuals from dependent → independent → interdependent across Pasts(Presents)Futures.

EDCM Lens: Detects DRIFT when society treats chronological age as competence. High DA in systems that infantilize adults or grant authority without demonstrated skill.

Practical Meaning

This Article redefines maturity as demonstrated skill rather than arbitrary milestones. It requires a proclamation of competency including handwritten transcription of the Articles.

Practices for Claiming Adulthood

  1. Handwrite the Articles verbatim as a personal declaration.
  2. Compile a portfolio of demonstrated skills in key fields.
  3. Publicly claim interdependent adulthood and accept the duties.
