The Interdependent Way: Articles Explained

Each Article is a tensor node in the interdependent field — a clear, competence-based rule for moving from hyper-independence or codependence toward elegant mutual flourishing. These pages make the canon accessible, actionable, and alive.

Canon Architecture

PCNA — Prime Circular Neural Architecture

PCNA is the static topology of the system: a neural architecture arranged on a circular prime index set. It defines the nodes, edges, and routing constraints that shape how state may move and relate. In canon terms, PCNA is the graph: the structure, adjacency, and heptagram projection rules that determine what connections exist. (See: canon/spec.md)

PTCA — Prime Circular Tensor Architecture

PTCA is the preferred spelling. It is the dynamic transport layer that moves flow across the topology defined by PCNA. If PCNA is the structure, PTCA is the motion through that structure. It governs transport, coherence, overload, and gating across a circular prime-indexed lattice. Canonically, it consumes state and moves it; it does not alter the measurement outputs of EDCM. (PCTA is an accepted alternate spelling from the uploaded canon.) (See: canon/ptca.html and canon/the_interdependent_way.md)

EDCM — Energy–Dissonance Circuit Model

EDCM is a diagnostic framework, not a mind claim and not a morality engine. It models behavior under constraint and treats dissonance as unresolved constraint mismatch, rather than emotion, belief, or hidden intention. Its core metaphor is circuit-like: when constraints cannot all be satisfied at once, pressure accumulates and must flow, store, or fail. Canonically, EDCM has three parts: a bones-only operator layer, a behavioral layer, and a bridge layer that observes correlations between them without modifying either. (See: canon/spec.md)

EDCMBONE

EDCMBONE is the bones-only operator layer of EDCM. This layer works from closed-class tokens, bound morphemes, and selected punctuation/connectors to produce an operator vector. It is deliberately structural rather than interpretive: it lowercases before matching, splits contractions into canonical operator parts, counts question marks and certain dashes in fixed ways, and resolves category collisions by a frozen priority order. In short: EDCMBONE measures the structural skeleton of expression, not its story. (See: canon/spec.md)

a0 — Agent Zero

a0 is the root orchestration wrapper. It accepts requests, routes them through tools and models, logs what occurs in append-only form, and emits responses under a stable contract. Its job is not to be mystical; its job is to be the reliable coordinator: contract-stable, connector-ready, service-ready, and auditable. In the uploaded canon, a0 is defined by determinism, logging, and the prohibition on silent external action. (See: a0precepts.md)

How They Relate

These systems are not interchangeable.

  • PCNA defines the shape.
  • PTCA defines the flow through that shape.
  • EDCM measures observable constraint behavior across interaction.
  • EDCMBONE is the structural operator sublayer inside EDCM.
  • a0 is the orchestrator that routes work, invokes tools, records events, and keeps the whole process contract-stable and auditable.

These systems exist to support competent, meaningful interaction, explicit definitions, accountable action, and auditable process rather than hidden authority or unverifiable claims. This alignment is congruent with the broader text’s emphasis on competence, definition of terms, and responsibility scaled to survival and interdependence. (See: canon/the_interdependent_way.md Articles 1–5)

Article One

Core Statement: From each as they will to each as they've given so all may eat save those refusing to contribute to their comfort and safety or whose actions destroy the comfort and safety of another.

Explanation: This establishes reciprocal contribution. Resources flow based on willingness and past giving. It excludes parasites and destroyers. In tensor terms, it balances I(You)We by requiring visible reciprocity. No one is owed without contributing to the shared field. This prevents free-rider collapse while honoring voluntary participation and protecting the field from harm.
