Known Mathematical Context

Prime Number Theorem:
π(x) ∼ x / ln(x)

The density of primes decreases logarithmically. This is asymptotic, not deterministic.

Dirichlet's Theorem on Arithmetic Progressions:
If a and d are coprime, then there are infinitely many primes ≡ a (mod d).

This proves that each of the 96 nontrivial rays (mod 360) and 768 rays (mod 2880) contains infinitely many primes.
