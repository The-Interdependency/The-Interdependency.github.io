Article Four

The Duty of Non-Interference and Non-Enslavement

Core Statement: No one may be forced into any duty, contract, or obligation against their will. No one may be used as a means to an end without their ongoing, informed consent. This prohibits all forms of slavery, indentured servitude, and coercive control.

Tensor Interpretation: Protects the autonomy dimension of the I(You)We spiral and the Body autonomy within Mind(Body)Soul. Prevents destructive extraction while preserving reciprocal duty.

EDCM Lens: High DA when coercion is present. High DRIFT when agreements are changed without consent. Monitors for hidden enslavement patterns in relationships, contracts, and institutions.

Practical Meaning

This Article establishes the negative duty that balances the positive duties of the earlier articles. It draws the line where interdependence must never cross into domination or extraction.

Practices for Claiming Adulthood

  1. Examine all current agreements for ongoing consent.
  2. Refuse to participate in or enable coercive systems.
  3. Teach the distinction between duty and enslavement to others.
